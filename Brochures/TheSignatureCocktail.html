  The
Signature
Cocktail
In 1934, famed barman
Fernand Petiot perfected the
recipe for a
vodka-and-tomato-juice
cocktail, known as the Bloody
Mary, at The St. Regis New
York’s King Cole Bar. After
nearly 90 years, the iconic
Bloody Mary remains the
signature cocktail of
St. Regis—uniquely crafted to
reflect the flavor of each locale.
The Bloody Mary
 
Ingredients:
 1 oz / 30 ml premium vodka
11 oz / 325 ml Bloody Mary mix
1 lemon wedge, for garnish
 
The Signature Bloody Mary Mix:
 Juice of 3 lemons
2 ½ #10 cans /2 ½ large tomato juice
5oz / 150ml Worcestershire sauce
10 dashes / 3ml Tabasco sauce
2tbsp / 30g freshly ground black pepper
2tbsp / 30g ground cayenne pepper
1tbsp / 15g ground celery salt
2tbsp / 30g whole black peppercorns
